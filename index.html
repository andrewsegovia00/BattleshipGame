How To Play

Your objective is to shoot down 3 battleships.

  • Main ship: 5 squares
  • Medium Cruiser: 4 squares
  • Small ship: 3 squares

Click any of the squares on the right-hand-side board to select your shot

OR

Write a selection between A-G (Ver) and 0-6 (Horiz)

*There's cool background music, just press the -PLAY BUTTON- at the top-right corner.
